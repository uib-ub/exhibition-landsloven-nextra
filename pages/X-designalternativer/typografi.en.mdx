# Design


## Typography

The fonts have been chosen to resemble those used in the exhibition. Alegreya is a classic font that is well suited for long texts, while Alegreya Sans is a simpler font that is well suited for navigation and other small text. MedievalSharp is a font that goes well with quotes and other special text.

We are far from having great similarity, but since we don't pay for fonts, it's the best we can do.

## Colors

The colors are used like other colors in Tailwind. Common ways of using the colors are `text-ll-blue-700 bg-ll-gold-200`. If you do not add a weight, e.g. `bg-ll-gold`, "DEFAULT" will be used. `ll' stands for "Landsloven", and the colors are based on those used in the exhibition.
